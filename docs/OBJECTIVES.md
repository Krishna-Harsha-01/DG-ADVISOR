# Objectives

1. Provide a simple input form where staff enter shipment goods and details and receive
   clear, structured dangerous-goods compliance guidance from an AI model.
2. Auto-detect the goods category and (where confidently known) the real UN number from
   the product name, while allowing manual override and verification against the SDS.
3. Store every generation in a central database with its inputs, AI response, provider,
   and response time, and make it searchable through a history view.
4. Control access with shift-based staff codes and an admin account, and record login and
   logout times so management knows who was working and when.
5. Give administrators a real-time analytics dashboard (usage per day, goods-type
   breakdown, average quality rating, and response time) to monitor adoption and output
   quality — with the tool clearly positioned as advisory, not a certified DG authority.
